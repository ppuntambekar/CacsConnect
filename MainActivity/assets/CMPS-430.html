Course Introduction The goal of this course is to give students a clear understanding of the architecture and organization of modern computers,
and the cost and performance tradeoffs involved in their design. The emphasis is on the major component subsystems of high performance computers.
Topics to be covered
Instruction Set Architecture
Processor
Pipelining
Memory Hierarchies
Multicore Processor
Interconnection Networks
Multicore Programming
